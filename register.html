Weekend School Registration Cost Per Year:

  • ISCJ Membershipfee : $425 per family
  • Tuition per child : $210
  • Expansion/Operations fee per child: $100 PTA fees: $20
  • Discount per child: $40 (starting from the third child)
  • ***For all Credit Card payments a transaction fee will be charged ***


Registration fees include all in-class handouts, study guides, textbooks and workbooks. Lunch is not included. Students must bring their own bag lunch.


Payment Breakdown:

  • 1 Child: $755.00 = $425 + $210 + $100 + $20
  • 2 Children: $1065.00 = $425 + $420 + $200 + $20
  • 3 Children: $1335.00 = $425 + $630 + $300 + $20 - $40 (discount)
  • 4 Children: $1605.00 = $425 + $840 + $400 + $20 - $80 (discount)
  • 5 Children: $1875.00 = $425 + $1050 + $500 + $20 - $120 (discount)
  • 6 Children: $2145.00 = $425 + $1260 + $600 + $20 - $160 (discount)

Yes, we offer a multi-child discount. It is already included in the Registration Cost Information and will be automatically applied when you check-out on the online registration form.

REGISTRATION IS IN PERSON THOUGH...?

Yes, on a case by case basis. If you need financial assistance, you will need to fill out the form using the link below. Financial assistance applications are reviewed on a case by case basis and we will ask you to meet in person/phone call or provide some documentation which may include proof of income (tax returns, pay stubs etc.) and/or any other relevant documents you feel would help us make a decision. You must complete the form before you register in order to receive the financial aid.

INSERT FORM...?

If you need a payment plan you can choose the option to pay in installments during your online registration. The payment plan we offer is 4 equal installments to be paid over a period of four months.
Payment plans by default will be auto-enrolled to deduct the remaining balance from your selected method of payment on the dates specified above.
