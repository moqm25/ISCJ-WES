BRAND NEW BOOKS!

ISCJ Weekend School has selected new books from the Weekend Learning Series. These engaging, colorful, interactive and informative books are supplemented by rich online annotated teacher's editions, online learning materials and student workbooks. Our teachers are being trained by an executive communications skills expert to ensure optimum virtual engagement for students. Our course materials are being loaded online. All of these improvements are being done with the objective of providing a high-quality Islamic education for your child.
